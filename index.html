How to Use This Tool

        
Update your Canvas course homepage in four steps.

      

      

        
Steps

        

          

            
1

            
Use the sections on the left to fill in your information — instructor details, course description, link labels and URLs, week page links, and course objectives.

          

          

            
2

            
Click "Generate HTML →" in the sidebar (or go to the Copy HTML tab) to build your code.

          

          

            
3

            
Click "Copy to Clipboard."

          

          

            
4

            
In Canvas, open your course homepage, click Edit, then click the </> HTML Editor button (top right of the toolbar). Select all the existing code, paste your new code, and click Save.

          

        

        

          Tip: Week URLs are found by navigating to the week's page in Canvas and copying the address from your browser's address bar.
        

      

      

        
Accessibility Notes

        
The generated HTML meets WCAG 2.1 AA standards: decorative images use empty alt="", layout tables include role="presentation", all color contrast ratios exceed 4.5:1, and links use descriptive text rather than "click here."
